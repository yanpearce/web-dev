Five Games Review

Rocket League

Rocket League is without a doubt my favorite game and I have spent over 2160 hours or 90 days in the game. At first glance, Rocket League seems to ba a simple game that uses cars to play soccer, which is true. Despite its simple nature, it is considered one of the hardest games in the gaming pool. The key reason is its tri-axis movement that allows players to control every micomovement done by their car. This allows for so many variation in shots and "mechanics", the game's skill moves but done by you, not a button. Due to the possibilities, it makes the game intriguing and accomplishing when you learn a skill. Rocket League is a easy must try and a great game.

Doom: Dark Ages

Doom itself is a multigame series where you play as a demon hunter fueled by rage and revenge for his lost bunny. Although he has a funny purpose, Doomslayer, the main character you play as, is arguably one of the strongest game characters. The game is a first person shooter that allows you to eradicate sworms and entire armies of demons with the coolest soundtrack from any game ever. It is a must play for those who want to feel pure masculinity and max testerone release.

War Hammer 40k: Space Marines 2

This is another game that I had treasured playing and gave a similiar experience to Doom. In general, you play in a multiworld area as giant hybrid humans that are in mech suits. It uses both guns and melee weapons, which allows for many different ways to eleminate targets and eradicate the aliens and heretics. The main purpose is to serve the great Emperor and Omnissiah by protecting different planets from Xenos raids. Another enemy as mentioned before are heretics, who are corrupted by Chaos Force and oppose the ways of the Emperor. There is over 100+ books on the story behind War Hammer so there is a ton to unfold and look at.

Grounded

The preface for Grounded is "Honey, I Shrunk the Kids." You must survive the yard as the size of ladybugs and find a way to make the embiggening cocktail to get back to normal size. It is a four player game that has a huge open world, huge being a yard, that is filled with many secrets. You can make armor and weapons using bug parts and other mini resources like gum or rust. The number of different classes and builds gives the player a lot of variety to conquer the yard.

Minecraft

One of the most nostalgiac and loved games on every device is Minecraft. From building dirt houses to fighting Ender Dragons, this blocky paradise is full of treasured memories and good laughs. Although the game is focused more towads kids and can become repetitive, it is good to hop on and play some more of it. It is a huge open world game with nearly infinite amounts of different worlds or "seeds". Many possiblilties can come from Minecraft with some of the best can make computers or a recreation of cities. The possibilities are endless and it is truly a game that will forever remain in the history books.
