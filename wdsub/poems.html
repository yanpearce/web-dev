Written by: Nikolas Pearce

A device that brings pain to its user
The same person that is the chooser
A machine that never fails to lure
Giving satisfaction and a cure
But don't be fooled by it's "Truth"
Put in place only to sooth

PHONES wear a mask meant to please
Easy to access and is used with ease
PHONES bring a new and false reality
Stripping the user of some of their vitality
PHONES may have a clever disguise
But I'm here to tell of it's lies

PHONES LIE about all of THE benefits
Truly HURTING those that are into IT
PHONES sneak INTO users MINDS
Making THE BRAIN put up the BLINDS
THOSE swept in BY it's BOAST
Are THOSE that ARE abused the MOST

BREAK IT'S CYCLE AND BECOME SANE
IT MAKES LIFE SO MINUTE AND MUNDANE
IT IS ONLY YOU THAT YOU CAN BLAME
FOR GETTING CAUGHT UP IN IT'S SILLY GAME
FIX YOUR POSTURE AND THE ADDICTION
SNAP INTO REALITY AND OUT OF FICTION

GO HOME
